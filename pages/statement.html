Statement

My research is based on the obser- vation of the refractions of light, in particularly those on the surface of the water, the change of direction and the waves that create and generate a hypnotic effect.

I am interested in exploring layers, their distortions and contrasts, that continuous transformation of forms that become a metaphor for the constant changes that we live by growing, evolving and therefore redefining ourselves.
